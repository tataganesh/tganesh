Biography

I am a Master’s student in the Department of Computing Science at the University of Alberta. Previously, I worked as a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Capillary Technologies. As part of the Instore Vision team at Capillary, I have worked on Computer Vision products for extracting useful insights about customers in an offline store, under the guidance of Subrat Panda and Sumandeep Banerjee. Before that, I was a Member Technical Staff at Stride.ai where I worked on Document Image processing techniques to extract metadata from PDF documents, and Optical Character Recognition for scanned documents, supervised by Pratheek Mahishi.
